# Research Notes

## Initial Assumption
People spend too much time searching and comparing products before buying.

## Problem Hypothesis
Users feel overloaded by too many choices and do not always know which product details matter.

## Competitor Areas to Review
- Amazon
- Wirecutter
- chat-based shopping assistants
- comparison tools

## Research Questions
- What makes online shopping frustrating?
- What information do users need before buying?
- Do users trust AI recommendations?
- What makes a recommendation feel useful?
